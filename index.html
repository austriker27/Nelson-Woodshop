About Me

Having grown up in the lush, rolling, tree filled hills of Tennessee my love for being in the outdoors and smelling the fresh pine smell of trees started from a young age. Just over a decade ago, I settled down in the Paciic Northwest with my family after a stint living abroad in Vietnam. I took up the time honored tradition of woodworking several years ago and hope my craftmanship will honor God and others.

Contact

I would love to build you anything you desire out of wood. Please reach out to me via this contact form and we will be in touch. I source only responsibly logged wood from local locations. Once you reach out, I can provide an estimate of the project investment.
